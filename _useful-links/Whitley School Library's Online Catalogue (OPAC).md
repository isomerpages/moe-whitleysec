### Whitley School Library's Online Catalogue (OPAC)

For Whitley Secondary staff and students, you may use this link [https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/whitleysec/](https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/whitleysec/) or the QR code below to search for your book titles, check on your library loans and loan history.

<img src="/images/opacqr.png" style="width:40%">

**Log-in instructions for staff**  <br>
*   You can make use of MIMS  
      
    

**Log-in instructions for students**<br>
*   Log-in ID: NRIC number
*   Password: Moe@DDMMYYYY (Your birthdate is 1 January 2010: E.g. 01012010)
